# 🎥 YouTube AI Summarizer Bot — Demo Script (4-5 Minutes)

This script is designed to showcase the bot's features, from personalization to native multi-language support.

---

## ⏱️ [0:00 - 0:45] Intro & Personalized Greeting
*   **Scene**: Open Telegram and find the bot.
*   **Action**: Type `/start`.
*   **Point**: "Notice the personalized greeting: *'Welcome, [Your Name]!'* We've removed all branding like 'Powered by Groq' to keep the interface clean and professional."
*   **Point**: "The bot immediately shows a list of commands and a quick guide."

## ⏱️ [0:45 - 1:30] Initial Search (English)
*   **Scene**: Copy a YouTube link and paste it into the bot.
*   **Action**: Send the link.
*   **Point**: "The bot shows a 'typing' indicator while it fetches the transcript and generates the summary. No more messy status messages like 'Fetching...' or 'Generating...'."
*   **Point**: "Look at this structure: Video Title, 5 Key Points, Important Timestamps, and a Core Takeaway. It's clean and easy to read."

## ⏱️ [1:30 - 2:30] Switching to Native Language (Telugu/Hindi)
*   **Scene**: Show the bot's multi-language power.
*   **Action**: Type `Summarize in Telugu` (or `Summarize in Hindi`).
*   **Point**: "This is where it gets powerful. The bot detects the language request and **automatically** regenerates the summary in that language."
*   **Point**: "Notice the headers—'Key Points', 'Timestamps'—they are now **100% translated** into the native script. No English words leaking into your native summary."

## ⏱️ [2:30 - 3:30] Native UI & Fast Actions
*   **Scene**: Tap the inline keyboard buttons.
*   **Action**: Click the 'లోతైన విశ్లేషణ' (Deep Dive) button.
*   **Point**: "See the buttons? They are also fully translated. No English 'Deep Dive' button here. Everything is localized."
*   **Point**: "The status message *'లోతైన విశ్లేషణ నడుస్తోంది...' (Running deep dive...)* also appears in your language. It’s a seamless, 100% native experience."

## ⏱️ [3:30 - 4:15] Q&A Capability
*   **Scene**: Ask a follow-up question in the native language.
*   **Action**: Type a question about the video in Telugu (e.g., "ఈ వీడియోలో ముఖ్యమైన విషయం ఏమిటి?").
*   **Point**: "You can talk to the bot just like a human. It knows the context of the video and answers intelligently in the same language you used."

## ⏱️ [4:15 - 5:00] Reset & Wrap-up
*   **Scene**: Clear the history for a fresh start.
*   **Action**: Type `/reset`.
*   **Point**: "When you're done, use `/reset` or `/clear` to wipe your history and start fresh. The bot confirms the session is cleared in your selected language."
*   **Point**: "And that's the YouTube AI Summarizer Bot—Personalized, Native, and Powerful."

---

### 🎙️ Voiceover Tips:
- Keep the tone energetic and focused on "Zero English leakage" for native users.
- Highlight the **speed** of the Groq LLaMA models.
- Emphasize that the bot remembers the video context during the Q&A phase.
